Eboni Brown

My name is Eboni and I am data analyst with a passion for harnessing the power of data to drive insights and informed decision-making. With a strong foundation in programming languages such as Python, SQL, and VBA, I bring a versatile skill set to the table, enabling me to dive deep into data analysis and visualization

I've honed my skills in various tools and packages, including pandas, matplotlib, numpy, Google Sheets and Tableau, to transform raw data into actionable insights. I'm driven by a commitment to efficiency and automation, exemplified by my successful implementation of VBA/macros to streamline processes, significantly reducing processing times and eliminating errors.

My experience as a Corporate Trust Operations Specialist at Wilmington Trust has equipped me with a meticulous eye for detail and a knack for problem-solving.

I'm also an excellent communicator and collaborator, having worked closely with cross-functional teams to achieve project goals. My ability to convey complex data findings through data storytelling has proven invaluable in driving informed decision-making.

In addition to my professional experience, I've continually expanded my skill set, earning certifications such as the Data Analyst Associate by DataCamp and actively participating in Data Analytics and Visualization Virtual Experiences. I am currently continuing to expand my data analytics knowledge through the American Dream Academy where I am working towards completing the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate.

My educational background includes a Data Science Bootcamp from Coding Dojo, a degree in Economics from the University of Delaware, and coursework in Mathematics from Howard University.

As a data enthusiast, I'm excited to take on new challenges and contribute my expertise to data-driven endeavors. Connect with me via email at ebonibrown2017@gmail.com or find me on LinkedIn and GitHub to explore potential collaborations and opportunities.
